Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide
pasta sheets with rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy
ricotta or béchamel, and lots of melted cheese. Baked
until golden and bubbly, its a comforting, hearty meal
enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles 12-16 sheets
  • Onion 1 medium, chopped
  • Mozzarella cheese 2 cups, shredded
  • Fresh basil or parsley 1/4 cup, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add 1 chopped onion and sauté until translucent (about 3-4 minutes).
  3. Add 2-3 minced garlic cloves and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Let the sauce simmer on low heat for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
