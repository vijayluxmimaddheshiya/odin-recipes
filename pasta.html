Mushroom Pasta

Mushroom pasta is a rich and flavorful dish made with sautéed mushrooms,
garlic, and herbs, tossed with pasta in a creamy or olive oil-based
sauce. The earthy flavor of mushrooms pairs perfectly with the
smooth sauce, creating a comforting and satisfying meal

Ingredients

  • Pasta – 200g (spaghetti, fettuccine, or your choice)
  • Mushrooms – 2 cups, sliced (button, cremini, or any variety)
  • Garlic – 2-3 cloves, minced
  • Olive oil or butter – 2 tablespoons
  • Heavy cream – 1/2 cup (for a creamy sauce)

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil or butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add 2 cups of sliced mushrooms and cook for 5-7 minutes until golden and tender.
  3. Stir in 2-3 minced garlic cloves and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
