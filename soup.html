Veg Soup

Vegetable soup is a hearty and nutritious dish made by simmering.
a variety of fresh vegetables in a flavorful broth.
Packed with vitamins and minerals, it can include ingredients .
like carrots, celery, potatoes, tomatoes, peas, and leafy greens.

Ingredients

  • Carrots – 2, chopped
  • Onion – 1, chopped
  • Tomatoes – 2, diced (or 1 can of diced tomatoes)
  • Garlic – 2 cloves, minced

Steps :

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until softened and fragrant.
  3. Add 4 cups of vegetable broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a simmer
  4. Let the soup simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with more salt, pepper, or herbs if needed.
