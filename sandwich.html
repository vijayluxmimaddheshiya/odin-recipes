Sandwich

A mozzarella sandwich is a simple yet delicious dish made with
fresh mozzarella cheese, often paired with tomatoes, basil, and
a drizzle of olive oil or balsamic glaze. The sandwich can be made
with toasted bread, like ciabatta or sourdough, giving it a crunchy
texture that complements the soft, creamy mozzarella

Ingredients

  • Fresh mozzarella 4-6 slices
  • Tomato 1, sliced
  • Basil leaves â€“ a handful
  • Ciabatta or sourdough bread 2 slices

Steps

  1. Lightly toast 2 slices of ciabatta or sourdough bread until golden and crispy.
  2. Place 4-6 slices of fresh mozzarella on one slice of the toasted bread.
  3. Add 1 sliced tomato on top of the mozzarella.
  4. Layer a handful of fresh basil leaves over the tomatoes.
